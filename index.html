About Jim

Explorer. Author. Truth Seeker.

Jim Vieira is a stonemason, researcher, and star of the History Channel's "Search for the Lost Giants." His groundbreaking work has uncovered evidence of ancient civilizations, mysterious megalithic structures, and compelling accounts of giants throughout history.

With decades of firsthand exploration and meticulous research, Jim has become one of the leading voices in alternative archaeology, challenging conventional narratives about humanity's past and revealing the hidden mysteries that orthodox history has overlooked.

Featured on numerous television documentaries and conferences worldwide, Jim continues to share his discoveries and inspire others to question what we think we know about our ancient ancestors.

Giants on Record
